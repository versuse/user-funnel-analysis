04 — Recommendations
01
Fix the App Open → First Order experience first.
Before adding incentives or changing acquisition spend, understand why engaged users aren't converting. Is there a clear first action after onboarding? Is the path to purchase obvious? This is a UX problem before it's a marketing problem.
02
Time-triggered nudges at the 48-hour mark.
Users who open the app but don't order within 48–72 hours almost never will. Trigger a push notification or email at that exact window — not a generic message, but a direct path to the most popular item or a limited-time offer tied to their browse history.
03
Scale the incentive — but measure retention, not just conversion.
Group B's uplift is real enough to test at scale. Track whether incentivised first-order users return at the same rate as organic converters. If retention drops, you're buying activations, not building users.
04
Invest in Referral before scaling Ads.
If live data confirms Referral conversion is close to Ads at lower CAC, that's where growth spend should shift. Referral programs compound. Ad spend doesn't.
Users are willing to open the app.
The product isn't giving them a reason to buy.
Fix that window, and the rest of the funnel follows.
